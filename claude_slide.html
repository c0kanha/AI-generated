How LLMs Work

Next-token prediction that learned to reason.

Capabilities

  • Learn by predicting the next token across trillions of words, developing emergent abilities at scale
  • Multi-step reasoning, code generation, translation, and passing medical, legal, and math exams
  • Develop internal world models — research shows linear representations of truth, space, and time
  • Express calibrated uncertainty and refuse to answer when confidence is low

Limitations

  • Can hallucinate — generating plausible but incorrect information with high confidence
  • Knowledge is static after training unless connected to external tools or retrieval systems
  • Struggle with truly novel problems far outside the training distribution
  • Fluency can create a false sense of authority, requiring human verification on critical tasks

LLMs are trained on next-token prediction, but what emerges is far more than autocomplete. They reason, plan, and create — while still being tools that work best when paired with human judgment.

The mechanism is simple. What emerges from it is extraordinary.
