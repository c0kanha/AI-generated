Actions & Stages

Action

Authorization

The issuing bank's approval (or decline) of a transaction. Holds the funds but does not transfer them yet.

click to explore →
Action

Capture

The step that converts an authorization into an actual charge. Often happens at end-of-day in a batch process.

click to explore →
Action

Settlement

The final transfer of funds from the payer's bank to the merchant's acquiring bank. Takes 1–3 business days.

click to explore →
Action

Batch Processing

Grouping multiple captures and submitting them to the processor at once, usually at end of day.

click to explore →
Action

Direct Debit

A payment method where the merchant is authorized to pull funds directly from your bank account.
